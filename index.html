Annie Feng

Hello, welcome! I'm a Master's of Engineering student at MIT CSAIL in the Learning and Intelligent Systems Group, advised by Tomas Lozano-Perez.

I graduated with my Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from MIT in 2023. I previously interned as an AI (perception) software engineer at NVIDIA. I am graduating in December 2024 with a concentration in Artificial Intelligence and am looking for full-time software engineering jobs.

My thesis work is at the intersection of large language models, task planning for robotics, and relational model-based reinforcement learning. I believe that high-level reasoning and planning methods leveraging foundation models is on the path to general-purpose robotics.
