Hello, welcome to my project page! I'm a Master's of Engineering student at MIT CSAIL in the Learning and Intelligent Systems Group, advised by Tomas Lozano-Perez. My thesis work is at the intersection of large language models, task planning, and relational model-based reinforcement learning.

I graduated with my Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from MIT in 2023. I previously interned as a software engineer at NVIDIA.
